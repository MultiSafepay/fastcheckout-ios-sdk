Requirements

  • Xcode 10.1 and iOS 12 SDK
  • iOS 9+ target
  • Swift 4.2

App Transport Security

iOS 9 introduces new security requirements and restrictions. So your app must comply with Apple’s App Transport Security policy.

To connect to Fastcheckout you need to add this ATS exception to your Info.plist file:

<key>NSAppTransportSecurity</key>
    <dict>
        <key>NSExceptionDomains</key>
        <dict>
            <key>multisafepay.com</key>
            <dict>
                <key>NSIncludesSubdomains</key>
                <true/>
                <key>NSExceptionRequiresForwardSecrecy</key>
                <false/>
            </dict>
        </dict>
    </dict>

Bitcode

The Fastcheckout iOS SDK works with apps that have bitcode enabled.

Keychain

To be able to store and retrieve data from the Keychain, you have to add to your app Capabilities Keychain Sharing.
